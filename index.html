Oracle Bookmarklets

Decimal Hours to Days, Hours and Minutes Tooltip

Enable a tool on the current page that will display a tooltip of the converted time for any highlighted number.
Useful for converting decimal hours (e.g. 8.5h) to Days, Hours and Minutes (e.g. 1 days, 1h 30m)

Enable Decimal Hours Converter Tooltip (Bookmarket)
(To use, drag and drop this link to your favorites bar and click to enable. Highlight any number to see it converted to days, hours and minutes.)

Web Clock - Show Total Clocked-in Time

Display the total amount of clocked in time (rather than just the amount since you last clocked back in) on the Web Clock page.

Web Clock - Show Total Clocked-in Time (Bookmarklet)
(To use, drag and drop this link to your favorites bar and click to enable when on the "Web Clock" page. It will add the total number of hours worked that day next to your Elapsed Time.)

Time Card - Show Real Reported Hours

Display the full decimal time as well as the days, hours and minutes for the Reported Hours on the Time Card page.

Time Card - Show Real Reported Hours (Bookmarklet)
(To use, drag and drop this link to your favorites bar and click to enable when on the "Time Card" page. It will add the real reported hours in days, hours and minutes.)
